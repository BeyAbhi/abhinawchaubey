Profile

The Person Behind the BIM

I am a Strategic BIM and Information Management professional with over 10 years of hands-on experience leading digital governance across complex, large-scale construction programmes in the UK, Middle East, and South Asia.

My work sits at the intersection of technical rigour and human collaboration — translating complex information requirements into clear, enforceable governance frameworks that enable teams to deliver with confidence and predictability.

Holding BSI's highest dual professional designation — both Asset Information Professional (AIP) and Project Information Professional (PIP) — I bring a full lifecycle perspective to every engagement, from strategic planning through to asset handover and operation.

Whether defining BIM Execution Plans for a FIFA World Cup stadium in Qatar, coordinating 12 sub-consultants on a luxury Abu Dhabi development, or implementing ISO 19650-5 security protocols for Heathrow Airport — I focus on building systems that outlast the project.
